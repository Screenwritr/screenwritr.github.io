Screenwritr

Industry Standard Formatting + The Power of Google Docs = Awesomeness

Screenwritr is a Google Docs Add-On that enables you to format your screenplays into an easy-to-read, industry standard layout. The best part? Screenwritr works right inside of Docs, enabling you to use all of the amazing features you're used to without having to rely on a sub-par dedicated screenwriting application.
